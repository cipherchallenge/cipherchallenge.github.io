- Modern Applications of Cryptography by Dr. Youssef El Housni.

Join us for an enlightening keynote presentation that delves into the fascinating world of cryptography and its evolution from classical techniques to modern applications. This talk will provide a comprehensive overview of how cryptography has been traditionally used for encryption and digital signatures, and will then explore groundbreaking advancements that are shaping the future of secure communication and data protection.

We will begin by revisiting the foundational principles of cryptography, highlighting its critical role in safeguarding information. From there, we will transition to discussing some of the most exciting and innovative applications in the field today, including:

  • Homomorphic Encryption: Learn how this revolutionary technique allows computations to be performed on encrypted data without decrypting it, enabling secure data processing in cloud computing and beyond.
  • Zero-Knowledge Proofs: Discover how these proofs enable one party to prove to another that they know a value, without revealing any information about the value itself. We’ll explore real-world applications such as privacy-preserving transactions in blockchain technologies.
  • Multi-Party Computation (MPC): Understand how MPC allows multiple parties to jointly compute a function over their inputs while keeping those inputs private. We’ll discuss its applications in collaborative data analysis and secure voting systems.

Throughout the talk, we will provide concrete examples of how these cryptographic techniques are being utilized in popular platforms and technologies, including Playstation, TikTok, Apple, WhatsApp, and various blockchains such as Bitcoin and Ethereum. By the end of the session, attendees will have a deeper appreciation for the critical role cryptography plays in our digital world and the innovative ways it continues to evolve to meet new challenges.

This keynote is designed for students and professionals alike who are interested in the cutting-edge developments in cryptography and its practical applications in today’s technology landscape.
