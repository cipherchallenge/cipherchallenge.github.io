What is POAP?

The Proof of Attendance Protocol (POAP) turns precious moments into collectibles (NFT). Using blockchain technology, POAP tokenizes your memories, so they can last forever and be truly yours.

You can reserve a POAP using an email address. If reserved with an email, it’s linked to your email, and you can mint it to your Web3 wallet later, though it’s not required immediately. While you can reserve and manage POAPs with just your email, using a crypto wallet adds ownership and flexibility. You can mint your POAP to the blockchain at any time for permanent recording.
